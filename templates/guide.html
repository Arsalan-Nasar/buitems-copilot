How to use BUITEMS Copilot

Everything you can do, explained simply. It takes just a minute to learn.

What makes it special

Not just a chatbot

BUITEMS Copilot does things an ordinary chatbot cannot. It understands how you talk, does the mathematics itself, and looks out for you.

01

Speaks Roman Urdu

Type the way you talk. “Mera result dikhao” works just like English, and it replies in the same language.

02

What-If & Predictor

Ask what a final mark would give you, or what marks you need for a grade. It plans your results before the exam.

03

Always Accurate

It calculates every grade and GPA using the real BUITEMS rules. It never guesses your numbers.

04

Looks Out For You

It warns you about low attendance, pending fees, and missing results before they become a problem.

What is this

Your portal, made simple

BUITEMS Copilot is a smart assistant for BUITEMS students. Instead of clicking through many pages to find your result, CGPA, fees or attendance, you simply ask a question and get a clear answer in seconds. You can write in English or Roman Urdu, whichever feels easy.

Why we made it

Because the portal is hard to use

The portal holds everything, but it is slow and confusing. Your marks are spread across many pages, your CGPA is hidden inside tables, and fees and attendance are difficult to read. We built Copilot to give you a fast, clear, and friendly way to check your studies, any time.

How to use it

Just three easy steps

1

Type your question

Use the box at the bottom of the screen. Write the way you talk, for example “show my result” or “meri fees kitni baqi hai”.

2

Or tap a quick button

Above the box you will find quick buttons for Result, CGPA, Fees, Attendance and GPA Trend. Tap one for an instant answer.

3

Read, save, or share

Your answer appears as a clean card. On a result, tap Save as PNG to download it and share with friends.
