BUITEMS Fee and Scholarship Information

Fee Payment:
Students must pay their semester fee before the due date announced each term. A late fee is charged on payments made after the deadline. Fees can be paid through the designated bank challan.

Fee Concession:
Students with financial need may apply for a fee concession through the student affairs office. Applications require supporting documents and are reviewed each semester.

Merit Scholarships:
Students who achieve a high CGPA may qualify for a merit scholarship. Typically, a CGPA of 3.5 or above makes a student eligible for partial fee waivers. The exact percentage depends on the CGPA band and available funding.

Need-Based Scholarships:
Students from low-income families can apply for need-based financial aid. This requires submitting income proof and an application form to the financial aid office.

Refund Policy:
Students who withdraw within the first two weeks of a semester may be eligible for a partial fee refund as per university policy.
